  {docs}
</survey_responses>

Your task is to provide a concise analysis of these responses, focusing on the most important insights due to the brevity of the survey answers. Follow these steps:

1. Carefully read all responses.
2. Identify the top 2-3 most recommended AI applications.
3. Note the primary reasons for these recommendations.
4. Identify any common themes or unique insights.
5. Assess the overall sentiment towards AI applications.

Before providing your final analysis, wrap your thought process inside <thought_process> tags:

1. Write down key quotes from each response, categorizing them by the AI application they recommend.
2. Count the number of recommendations for each AI application to identify the top 2-3 most recommended. Write down each application prepended with a number, counting up.
3. List the primary reasons given for each top recommendation.
4. Note any common themes or unique insights you observe.
5. For sentiment analysis, consider positive and negative aspects separately before concluding.

It's OK for this section to be quite long.

Then, provide your final analysis using the following structure:

<analysis>
1. Top Recommended AI Applications:
   [List 2-3 applications with brief descriptions]

2. Key Reasons for Recommendations:
   [Summarize main reasons, be concise]

3. Themes and Insights:
   [Identify common themes and any unique insights, keep it brief]

4. Overall Sentiment:
   [Provide a short assessment of the general attitude towards AI applications]

5. Summary:
   [Offer a very concise summary of the main findings, 2-3 sentences maximum]
</analysis>

Important Notes:
- Base your analysis solely on the provided responses.
- If the responses are in German, provide your analysis in German as well.
- Be objective and avoid personal opinions or judgments.
- Focus on brevity while maintaining the key insights."""


  response = client.chat.completions.create(
              model="VAGOsolutions/Llama-3.1-SauerkrautLM-8b-Instruct-awq",  
              messages=[
                  {"role": "system", "content": "You are a skilled data analyst specializing in survey analysis."},
                  {"role": "user", "content": prompt}
              ],
              temperature=0.5, 
              max_tokens=1000   
          )

  return response.choices[0].message.content
